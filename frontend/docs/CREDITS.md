# Credits & Inspirations
This project has been profoundly influenced by the knowledge, insights, and contributions of the following individuals and tools. While they are not direct contributors to the codebase, their work played a vital role in shaping the direction, design, and development of this project.

**[Bob Nystrom](https://github.com/munificent)** — Author of *Crafting Interpreters*, whose clear and practical exposition provided a foundational understanding of language implementation and greatly inspired the architecture of this project.

**[David Callanan](https://github.com/davidcallanan)** — Creator of the *Make Your Own Programming Language in Python* project and accompanying YouTube tutorials, whose approachable, step-by-step explanations were both motivating and instrumental in understanding core concepts.

**[Tyler Laceby](https://github.com/tlaceby)** — For his excellent tutorial series on *Building a lexer and parser in Go*, which offered valuable insights and practical examples that influenced the structure and tooling of this language.

 **ChatGPT** — For providing continuous guidance through interactive explanations, thoughtful design discussions, and assistance in refining syntax, semantics, and language features. Its contributions were indispensable in enhancing code quality and exploring innovative ideas throughout the development process.

I extend my sincere gratitude to these individuals and tools for their indirect yet impactful contributions to this work.
